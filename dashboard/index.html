Key Finding
The balance-to-advance ratio is the single most predictive signal. Users requesting advances larger than their available balance default at 3.4× the rate of well-buffered users. This one feature accounts for 24% of the model's total predictive power.
Business Action
Users with 29+ days since last payroll AND overdraft frequency > 3 represent the highest-risk cohort. Tightening the advance cap to $15 for this segment captures 68% of defaults while affecting only 9% of total applications.
